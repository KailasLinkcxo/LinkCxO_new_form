Board-Level Insights
Revenue at 49%. Half the annual target is unachieved. Q4 pipeline conversion is the only lever left this year.
Sharp Q3/Q4 drop. Achievement fell from 94% in Q2 to 41.5% in Q3 and 20.4% in Q4. Closure acceleration is critical.
₹5Cr+ live pipeline. 37 active mandates remain open. Accelerating these is the highest-priority action.
43 mandates blocked. Nearly half of all mandates are on hold due to client-side decisions.
Single closer. Of 6 recruiters, only Diksha Sehgal shows meaningful joined revenue. Team-wide closure support is needed.
Retainer at 31%. Increasing retainer penetration secures upfront revenue and reduces dependency on closures.
